Trip Planning Guide

How to Travel

Most areas are accessible by bus, van, or jeep. Hire local guides for trekking areas.

Where to Stay

Guest houses and 3-star hotels are widely available in all major valleys.

Budgeting Tips

  • Travel in groups to split costs
  • Book in advance during summer
  • Use local transport for savings

Packing Essentials

  • Warm clothing and rain gear
  • Medicines and first aid
  • Power banks and torch
